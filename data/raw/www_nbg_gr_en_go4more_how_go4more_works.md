## Get rewards for your transactions 
Every contact you have with the bank offers you **Go For More** points. This way, you earn points for every transaction you carry out with us and not just for purchases with your cards. This is our way to say thank you for trusting us.
![How can I earn points?](https://www.nbg.gr/dist/nbgportal/browser/assets/images/default-image.png)
### How can I earn points?
By registering with the **Go For More** program you start earning points through your everyday transactions and purchases with NBG’s cards.

You can get informed any minute on the “[My Points](https://www.nbg.gr/en/go4more/your-go4more)” page, via [Digital Banking](https://ibank.nbg.gr/web/?loginType=retail), in the **Go For More** app, POS receipt, as well as in the branch of your choosing.

## Do you want more information?
What is Go For More?
It is the first comprehensive rewards program in the Greek market. Which means that you get rewards not just for the transactions you perform with NBG’s cards, but for every transaction you carry out with us. Form more information, click [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Forms-privacy-statements/go4more/terms-of-operation-go-for-more.pdf).
What benefits will I get with Go For More?
You get rewards for every transaction you carry out with us. This means that you earn points from deposits, loans, investments, as well as from transactions with NBG cards. This way, the more transactions you make the more you earn. Furthermore, you have flexibility with respect to the redemption of your points, as you can choose which of the 7.500 partner businesses you redeem your points at.
How can I register with the Go For More program?
You can register for free, using any of the following ways:
  * On the [Go For More registration](https://www.nbg.gr/en/go4more/how-go4more-works/registration) page
  * Via the **Go For More** app for iOS or Android
  * Via Digital Banking
  * By visiting any NBG branch
  * By calling at 210 48 48 484 from a fixed line or mobile phone


How can I earn points?
With NBG’s **Go For More** loyalty program, collecting points is simple! Just register for the program and then automatically earn points with your daily banking transactions, as well as with your purchases with NBG cards. The **Go For More** program was created for you, to reward you daily. 
You earn **Go For More** points when you: 
  * Make purchases with NBG cards
  * Carry out payments of debts / bills
  * Make bank transfers 
  * Buy and use financial products (bancassurance & investment products and deposit packages)
  * Keep up to date with your mortgage and consumer loan instalments


The more transactions and purchases you make, the more points you earn. Find out how you can earn more points every day [here](https://www.nbg.gr/en/go4more/your-go4more).
How can I redeem my points?
Using your debit, credit or prepaid card at the special terminals of the **Go For More** partner businesses (POS), you can choose to fully or partially redeem the points you have accumulated, and this way pay less or not at all.  
Supermarkets, gas stations, department stores, telephony & Internet, travel agencies, hotels, restaurants, health & beauty services, baby items, electronics & electrical items are just some of the options available to you!
See [all the partner businesses](https://www.nbg.gr/en/go4more/partners/cooperating-companies), as well as the stores closest to you.
  * Make sure that you make your transaction through NBG terminals (POS)
  * Remember that you need a minimum of 500 points for redemption and that the points you collect can be redeemed within 1 year from the date of collection!


Do points expire?
Yes, if you do not redeem them within 1 year from the date they were earned. However, when you redeem your points, the points collected at the earliest date will be the first ones to go.
How do I find out about offers?
You can keep up to date with the program offers on the [Go For More offers](https://www.nbg.gr/en/go4more/offers) page, the **Go For More** app, internet or mobile banking, and the **Go For More** Facebook page. You can also read our monthly **Go For More** newsletter to stay up to date.
Are there any limitations to the points I earn?
You can earn up to 500.000 points each year.
If I’m late on a loan payment, will I lose my points? 
You won't lose any **Go For More** points. According to the terms of the **Go For More** program, however, if any obligations are past due by one day or more, no redemption of **Go For More** points can be made.
The ability to redeem points is regained immediately after the respective obligations have been settled.
Can I use the Go For More app with any mobile phone?
So long as you have an Android or iOS mobile phone to download the application from Google Play, AppGallery or AppStore.
If I cancel any of my insurance policies, will I lose my points?
In case you cancel your insurance in any way, no refund of points will be made and the amount of unearned premiums, will be credited in the deposit account or to the credit card you used for the insurance.
