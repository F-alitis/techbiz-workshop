  * Reduce the fixed interest rate on your loan by 0.10% regardless of the energy category

  * Get a 50% discount on loan expenses and with the lowest fixed interest rate for 10 years*
  * Benefit from a repayment term that may reach up to 40 years


_*for applications submitted by 31/03/2026_

## Do you want more information? 
Is the information on mortgage loans available in the form of an electronic brochure?
Of course. You can download the electronic brochure by clicking [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Idiwtes/daneia/e-leaflet-mortgage-loan-international-buyers.pdf).
What is the loan amount I can get? 
The loan amount starts at a minimum of €10,000 and may finance up to 100% of the total cost of your investment - based on the purchase contract - or relevant project. 
In order to calculate the maximum you could borrow, we take under consideration parameters like the property value and the monthly repayments you can afford depending on your monthly income and standard expenditures. 
You should note that it will be necessary to make a down payment in the event of a home purchase. To get a better idea of the loan amount you can get, you can use our loan calculator to determine the right repayment plan for your circumstances. 
What can I use ESTIA Green for? 
With ESTIA Green you can get a loan to be used for:
  * A home purchase 
  * Land purchase for building a home 
  * Building, completing, expanding, or repairing a house 


How can I repay my monthly loan repayments? 
You can pay by monthly amortization installments, starting one month after the loan disbursement and payable via the borrower’s designated loan-linked Deposit Account with NBG.
Can I tailor monthly repayments to my needs? 
Yes, you can adjust monthly repayments by choosing:
  * An initial period of paying only interest during the first 1 or 2 years. 
  * After the second loan repayment year, you can skip one installment per year. 


How does the Bank notify me about my installments, loan rate, and other details? 
The Bank sends you a monthly notification regarding your housing loan, remaining principal, remaining installments, the interest rate on outstanding amounts, etc. Moreover, if you’re using NBG Internet Banking, you can register with Statements and receive the notifications via email. 
What is the interest rate of the loan? 
For ESTIA Green Home you can choose:
A floating interest rate based on 3M Euribor plus interest margin throughout the entire term of the loan or
A fixed interest rate for the first 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25 or 30 years and floating rate thereafter based on 3M Euribor plus interest margin.
Note that the floating interest rate is subject to changes in line with any increase or reduction in Euribor and this may cause your instalment amount to go up or down. Also note that the loan's interest rate is subject to the levy under Law 128/75, currently 0.12%.
You can find all the details regarding the loan rate and charges [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Timologio/web_portal_english_epitokia-timologio_daneiwn.pdf). Please note that the interest rate is calculated when your application is assessed, taking into account factors such as the purpose of the loan, the value of the mortgaged property and your credit score. 
However, ESTIA Green Home also offers you:
  * A discount on the fixed fee by 0.10% regardless of the energy category. For example, if the fixed rate margin is normally 2.90% and you are entitled to a 0.10% discount, the final fixed interest margin on your loan will be 2.80%.
  * Α discount on the floating rate margin. This means that you earn a reduction in your interest rate depending on the energy class of the house after the purchase or after the completion of construction or repair works. This reduction amounts to 0.25% for energy classes A and A+ , 0.15% for energy classes B and B+ and 0.10% for energy class C. For example, if the floating rate margin is normally 2.00% and you are entitled to a 0.25% discount, the final interest margin on your loan will be 1.75%.


When does the ESTIA Green discount rate start? 
The reduced interest rate (fixed and floating rate margin) applies to the following cases:
  * To buy a house, from the first instalment.
  * For other cases (purchase of a house under construction, construction/completion /renovation), after the completion of the works and the submission of the Energy Upgrade Certificate to the Bank, by which the final energy category is assigned to the property, as long as the loan is performing and, in the event of renovation, the house has been upgraded by at least 1 energy category.


How is the loan amount disbursed? 
The disbursement of the loan amount depends on the purpose of the loan. 
  * In the case of a home purchase, the loan amount is disbursed in a lump sum. 
  * In the case of construction, the loan amount is disbursed through partial disbursements, following the progress of your project. After the first payment, an engineer in partnership with the Bank will monitor the progress of the construction works before every subsequent payment. 


What is the repayment term? 
Up to 40 years, as long as the age of the youngest person participating in the loan and having a personal income does not exceed 75 years at the maturity of the loan.
Can I make an early repayment? 
Yes, you can make an early or partial repayment at any time without any additional charge.
What supporting documents do I need to apply for the loan? 
You can view [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Idiwtes/Forms-for-loans/dikaiologitika-stegastikon-daneiwn.pdf) all the supporting documents you will need. You can always visit one of our NBG branches and talk to our experienced staff for any additional information.
How can I change my personal details? 
You can update your personal details using your Online Banking codes. Through eGov-KYC, you can change your income figure, contact details, address, and profession, or you can upload the relevant verification documents. Alternatively, you can visit one of our NBG branches. You can learn more about all the relevant documentation in the [information brochure](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Idiwtes/generic/Have-you-introduced-if-not-do-it-now.pdf).
How can I calculate my monthly amortization installments? 
You can use our [mortgage calculator](https://www.nbg.gr/en/individuals/daily-transactions/online-banking-tools/mortgage-calculator) and calculate the monthly amortization installments that best suit your needs.
Do I earn Go For More reward points with ESTIA Green? 
Yes. Every time you pay your monthly installment in time, you earn **Go For More** reward points, provided you have a debit or credit card with NBG and have signed up for the program. 
Do I need collateral for the loan? 
Yes. The loan is granted subject to a mortgage prenotation on a property asset of sufficient collateral value under NBG’s current lending policy. For any related expenses, please read more information [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Timologio/web_portal_english_epitokia-timologio_daneiwn.pdf).
Do I need insurance? 
Yes, insurance coverage is required to protect your financed property against basic risks, such as fire and earthquake, pipe fracture/cracking, burglary, etc. 
You can choose any insurance company you want. Ethniki Insurance can undertake to protect your property at a particularly low premium, through quick and easy procedures. Specifically, you can have: 
  * a minimum property coverage plan 
  * a minimum and additional property coverage plan 
  * a content coverage plan. 


You can also opt, optionally, for borrower's Life insurance with a low premium from Ethniki Insurance, to ensure the repayment of the loan in case of unforeseen events (death due to illness or accident), without this being in any way mandatory.

What are the steps after the final approval? 
**Taking out a mortgage**
After signing the loan contract at the branch, it’s time to pay the mortgage prenotation expenses and initiate all relevant actions. An NBG associate lawyer undertakes to arrange the mortgage prenotation and obtain all relevant certification upon completing the process.   
  
**Loan disbursement**
As soon as all formal procedures are concluded, we’ll get in touch with you so you can notify us of the date you would like the loan to be disbursed to your account. If you’re taking out the loan for construction/completion or repairs, the subsequent loan amount is disbursed through partial disbursements, in line with the progress of your project. After the first payment, an engineer in contract with the Bank will monitor the progress of the construction works before every subsequent payment. 
Where can I find more information about NBG Housing Loans? 
For further details, you can read the General Information Document on NBG Housing Loans, which is available [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Idiwtes/Forms-for-loans/Genikes-plirofories-gia-stegastika-daneia.pdf).
What happens if I don’t get the loan statement on time? 
We send you the loan statement monthly to the address you’ve given us. If for any reason beyond the Bank’s control, the statement is not delivered to you, the installment will nevertheless still be due for collection, as required under the loan agreement. For further information, please visit your NBG Branch. 
What happens if I fail to pay an installment by the due date? 
It would be best if you endeavored to settle your dues as soon as possible. Failure to pay an installment incurs default interest for the period it remains in arrears, while it is also taken to indicate transactional delinquency. In addition, your loan agreement may stipulate further penalties.

To get the loan you want, you need to notify us of your personal details. We will need your ID card, passport and/or valid foreigner’s residence permit, depending on your nationality.
### Income verification 
You get a housing loan with the obligation to repay it. So, we will need your income tax statements for the last three years and documentation certifying your professional status, such as employer’s certificate and/or a copy of the latest salary statement and document evidencing your VAT status.
… If you’re a pensioner, you need to submit your latest pension payment slip. 
You will also need to submit property ownership documentation (ENFIA property tax clearance certificate or a printout of no property ownership status). 
For a complete list of all the required documentation, click [here](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Idiwtes/Forms-for-loans/dikaiologitika_stegastikon_daneiwn_en.pdf). 
[expand](https://www.nbg.gr/en/individuals/loans/mortgages/property-loans/estia-green)
### Loan limitations 
To get the housing loan you want, please help us evaluate your application properly. Please answer all our questions accurately and submit all the necessary documentation.
## About your loan application 
### Your first visit 
To start the process, you need to visit one of our branches and submit the necessary documentation and your application. If your application is preapproved, we will contact you to notify you and provide you with the European Standardised Information Sheet – Initial Offer detailing the loan… terms. You can provide your email for your convenience, and we will send you the documents digitally so you won’t need to visit the branch.[expand](https://www.nbg.gr/en/individuals/loans/mortgages/property-loans/estia-green)
### Verification 
After preapproval, you need to submit the necessary documents for the legal verification of property titles and technical appraisal of the property. Before the verification procedures take place, all [relevant expenses](https://www.nbg.gr/-/jssmedia/Files/Timologio/web_portal_english_epitokia-timologio_daneiwn.pdf) should be paid in full. Click here to learn more about the necessary documentation,… depending on the type of property.[expand](https://www.nbg.gr/en/individuals/loans/mortgages/property-loans/estia-green)
### Final approval 
Once all the verification procedures are concluded, your application gets evaluated, and final approval is given. You will then receive the European Standardised Information Sheet – Binding Offer and set a date for signing the loan contract. For better planning, please note that – by law – at least… five days must have lapsed from the day all the participants in the loan contract receive the Binding Offer before you can go ahead with signing it. Read more about the next steps in question "What are the steps after the final approval? ".[expand](https://www.nbg.gr/en/individuals/loans/mortgages/property-loans/estia-green)
