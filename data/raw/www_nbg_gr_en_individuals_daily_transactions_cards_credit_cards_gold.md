Do I have the option not to use the contactless functionality of my card without using a pin?
You can either reset or restore the applicable limit of contactless transactions carried out without the use of a pin, either through the Internet & Mobile Banking applications, as long as you are a registered user, or by submitting a request to his or her NBG Branch or by calling the Bank’s Contact Center. 
How can I activate my card? 
You can activate your card when you make your first transaction at an NBG ATM, by selecting "Card Activation" from the menu. For more information, we recommend that you watch the tutorial [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gZZM7bCzL3A) we created for you.
What should I do if I forget my PIN or it’s blocked?
If you forget your PIN or it’s blocked for some reason, you can easily retrieve it:
  * Via Internet Banking, from PIN Management by selecting Unlock PIN 
  * Via Mobile Banking, from Card Management by selecting Unlock PIN
  * By calling 210 48 48 484


What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
If you are a Digital Banking user you can:  

  * "Freeze" your card to temporarily stop its operation and in case it is found, reset it by selecting "Unfreeze"
  * Report loss/theft


Alternatively, you should contact the bank's Call Center directly at 210 48 48 484, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. 
Can I manage my card via Digital Banking?
You can easily manage your credit card via Digital Banking and do the following:  

  * Activate/deactivate the option to carry out cash withdrawals, contactless and/or online transactions, foreign transactions, etc.
  * Freeze/unfreeze
  * Notify the bank of loss/theft
  * Change the payment method (partial or full payment)
  * Link account


How many additional cards can I have at the same time?
You can issue up to 3 free additional cards and share your card benefits with your family members.
